cora.util.sphfunc¶

Special functions for calculations on the sphere.

These routines require pygsl to be installed. Although these functions are available in scipy they tend to be inaccurate at large values of l.

cora.util.sphfunc.Ylm_array(lmax, theta, phi)¶

  • theta (float or array_like) – Angular position.

Evaluate the spin-2 spherical harmonics.
